# energy-check

A brief self-assessment to surface how you are feeling about your work right now. Use it before starting a session, when you feel stuck, or whenever you want a low-friction check-in with yourself.

## When to use

Invoke at any point during a work session. Takes less than two minutes. No preparation needed.

## Prompt

Rate your current energy for this work on a scale of **1 to 5**:

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Depleted — this feels like obligation, not interest |
| 2 | Low — dragging, hard to start |
| 3 | Neutral — fine, steady |
| 4 | Engaged — interested and moving |
| 5 | Energised — absorbed and alive |

After you have your number, answer one follow-up question:

> **What is one small thing that would move your score one point higher right now?**

It might be a break, a different task, a shorter scope, music, or simply acknowledging that the work is good enough for today.

## Output

Output is advisory only. No other skill in this plugin reads or gates on this output.

Inline conversation text only. No file is written.
